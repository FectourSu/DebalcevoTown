GEOGRAPHY
        Distance to Donetsk: by road — 74 km. Distance to Kiev: by road-803 km, by rail-797 km.

        The border between Donetsk and Luhansk regions runs along the eastern outskirts of the city.

        The city is located on a hill, from which many rivers of the Donbass originate. In the southern outskirts of the city there is the source of the river called Bulavin (a tributary of the Krynka, the Minus basin). In the north-eastern surroundings — the source of the river called Lozovaya (a tributary of the Lugan River, the basin of the Seversky Donets). To the north is the source of the river called Sanzharovka (a tributary of the Lugani).

        To the northwest is the source of the river called Skelevaya (a tributary of the Lugani). On the western outskirts is the source of the river called Karapulka (a tributary of the Lugani).

TRANSPORT
         Debaltseve-buses intended for use as urban public linear (that is, route) passenger transport.
The city bus makes frequent stops, where it disembarks and takes on board a large number of passengers.

        Such a bus should have wide doors and passages, storage platforms, handrails for standing passengers. They can be represented by a special type — fixed-route taxis ("minibuses"), stopping at the request of passengers.

        A trackless mechanical motor vehicle technically designed to carry nine or more passengers and capable of maneuvering on the road, driven by an energy source stored or produced from fuel stored on board (there are battery, gasoline, gas-fuel, diesel, supercapacitor, as well as buses running on other fuel cells).
