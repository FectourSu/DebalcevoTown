ABOUT
        Debaltseve (Ukrainian: Debaltseve) is a city in the Donetsk region.


       Debaltseve is a city of regional subordination, located in the eastern part of the Donetsk region, in the center of the Debaltseve-Ivanovo ridge, which reaches its highest point here-334 meters above sea level. The distance to the regional center is 76 km. Debaltseve is the largest railway junction in the country.


       The city of Debaltseve was founded in 1878 as a railway station in connection with the construction of the Catherine Railway. The railway station building, built in 1879, has been preserved to this day, now it is an architectural monument. The name is associated with the adjacent villages of Ilyinskaya, which in the 19th century was granted to state councilor Ilya Nikolaevich Deboltsov for participating in the suppression of the Decembrist uprising. After some time, Ilyinka received her second unofficial name-Deboltsovka. This is how it was indicated on topographic maps of the 19th century, and the station, which was founded on the northern borders of the Deboltsov estate, was named Debaltsevo.
